Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about ROSBOTZ courses and sessions.

What age groups do you teach?

Our programs are perfectly mapped for students anywhere from Age 5 to 18+. We have specific pathways from beginner block-coding up to advanced C++ algorithms.

Do I need any prior experience?

Absolutely not! Our foundation levels assume zero prior knowledge. We slowly introduce complex concepts entirely from scratch.

How does the 1-to-1 session work?

You login via a Live Google Meet link. It is just the student and the dedicated mentor analyzing code and building physical robots together.

Do you ship robotics kits internationally?

Yes! We have reached students in over 30 countries and utilize global courier networks to deliver our hardware bundles tracking-enabled.

What payment methods do you accept?

We process secure payments globally via standard credit/debit cards, and accept local UPI frameworks for Indian residents.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. If you are not completely satisfied within the first week of initiating your curriculum, you receive a full refund minus hardware shipping constraints.
